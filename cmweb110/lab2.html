Some Neurodivergent Tools and Resources

  • Headspace Student Plan - Helps with anxiety and stress
  • Notion for Education - This can help students and teachers get organized which helps with ADHD
  • Thruday - This is another resource to help with ADHD
  • Tiimo - a tool built for people with autism and ADHD in mind
  • Goblin Tools - "A collection of small, simple tools, for when things feel too big or complicated" - From the Goblin Tools site
  • Habitica - A to do list site and app to gamify your tasks and to-dos
